## Migration Considerations

If you're currently using Beaconstac and considering a move to QRWolf, the migration path depends on how you're using the platform. Static QR codes don't require any migration—they encode destination URLs directly and will continue working regardless of which platform generated them.

Dynamic QR codes are platform-dependent because they route through the provider's redirect servers. Moving dynamic codes means recreating them on QRWolf and updating any printed materials that display them. This is an unavoidable friction point when changing providers, but it's also an opportunity to audit your QR code inventory and retire codes that are no longer serving their purpose.

For organizations with extensive Beaconstac integrations—CRM connections, workflow automations, team structures—migration becomes more complex. These integrations would need to be rebuilt or reconsidered. In these cases, the simplicity benefit of QRWolf may be offset by the cost of recreating ecosystem connections.
